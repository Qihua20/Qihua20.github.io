Senior Thesis Research – Bednarek Lab

I’m currently investigating the role of SCD2a cell's AP and clathrin binding sites in Arabidopsis membrane trafficking. This work involves site-directed mutagenesis (Y356A, L447A, L494A), vesicle trafficking analysis, and fluorescent microscopy.

  • Analyzing endocytic pathway disruptions in mutant Arabidopsis cells
  • Using gel electrophoresis and sequencing for validation
  • Documenting phenotypic outcomes and molecular interactions

Image source: "Arabidopsis Dividing Vesicle Dynamics" by Bednarek lab at bednareklab.biochem.wisc.edu/

Gopalan Lab Research

Techniques

Applications
