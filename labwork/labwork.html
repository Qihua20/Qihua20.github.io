Core Laboratory Techniques

Throughout my time in the Gopalan and Bednarek labs, I’ve developed strong technical skills in wet-lab and molecular biology research.

Experimental Skills

  • PCR & Gel Electrophoresis: For DNA amplification and visualization
  • TLC (Thin Layer Chromatography): For organic reaction monitoring
  • NMR Spectroscopy: For structural analysis of synthesized molecules
  • Fluorescent Microscopy: For imaging protein localization in Arabidopsis
  • Immunohistochemistry: To detect proteins using specific antibodies
